Module 1
Introduction
This module gets you started on understanding networking. You will be introduced to a fundamental network technology.

How communication takes place on the internet is truly extraordinary. Ever wonder what happens when you type in a web address and click “enter”? In just milliseconds your browser can show you an image from a computer thousands of miles away. In just a blink of an eye, hundreds and maybe thousands of computers are automatically moving data to the right destination.
You probably understand data is transmitted from somewhere else, but how it’s organized and routed through billions of copper cables, fibre lines and wireless transmissions can be credited to the invention of TCP/IP (Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol).

What you will learn throughout this module is how these fundamental technologies make the internet work and how computers understand where to send and receive data from your house to a datacenter far, far away.
